Title: Negotiate my job offer

----

Text: Don’t forget to negotiate. Again, don’t forget to negotiate. Why? First, it’s the easiest money you’ll ever make. Second, most employers expect you’ll negotiate. Typically, they’ll offer a lower salary / benefits package than they’re willing to provide. Finally, once you start the job, this golden opportunity is gone forever.

----

Get-personal: Ask a (link: https://hbr.org/2015/03/find-the-career-coach-whos-right-for-you text: career coach) or other skilled negotiator in your life for advice how to do this. Even if it costs some money, there’s a good chance it’s worth it; see link #1 above. The company has people who negotiate job offers all day long. Why shouldn’t you have expert help?
